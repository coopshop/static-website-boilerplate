Simple feedback popup

Toast

    You can show the toast on specific position with one of following classes.
    Default position to Bottom: toast--show
    Bottom: toast--show-bottom
    Bottom Left: toast--show-bottomleft
    Bottom Right: toast--show-bottomright
    Top: toast--show-top
    Top Left: toast--show-topleft
    Top Right: toast--show-topright
    <div class="toast toast--show-bottom">Simple feedback popup</div>
